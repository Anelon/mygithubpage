Wineries and Craft Breweries

Ideally situated in the Sierra Foothills, the wineries are thriving producing top notch vintage. The location is excellent for growing wine grapes, which ripen during hot summer days and cool in the night air from the Sierras.

Relaxation


Golf Opened in 1918, Wawona Golf Course was the first regulation course opened in the Sierra Nevada.

Spas Yosemite is all about the Great Outdoors, but that doesn’t mean you can’t enjoy great pampering. After a vigorous day of exploring, nothing beats a relaxing spa treatment.

Arts and Culture

While in Yosemite National Park visit the authentic art galleries featuring work from local and regional artists. In addition performances of the 50 piece Mariposa Symphony Orchestra and the summer concert series are an excellent way to experience the cultural attractions.

  • Art Galleries
  • Symphony Orchestra
  • Summer Concerts
  • Theatre
  • Chats with Rangers
  • Yosemite Historic Village
  • Historic Wawona Hotel
  • Museum
  • Native American Basket Weaving

3 Day Kings Canyon, Sequoia,

and Yosemite National Park

Sample Itinerary

Day 1

- Your professional tour guide will meet you when you arrive in the Central Valley and escort you to the beautiful Chateau du Sureau; a five star resort nestled in the Sierra Nevada foothills just a few miles outside of Yosemite National Park. You will spend your first two nights in the elegant Salon Suite of the Villa Sureau. Dine that evening in the five diamond restaurant; Erna’s Elderberry House located on site at Chateau du Sureau. Enjoy a private 3 coarse dinner perfectly paired with a Napa wine. Return to your suite and relax from your travels by sinking into a relaxing herbal bath while sipping sparkling wine.




Day 2

- Start your morning watching the sun peek over the vast vista of the Sierra Mountain Range while enjoying a steaming cup of coffee and a breakfast made to order. Your personal guide will escort you in your private SUV for your first day of exploring the “Jewel of the National Parks”, Yosemite! First on the list is the iconic and expansive view of Yosemite Valley from the view point that been seen, photographed, painted and drawn by internationally renowned artists to casual tourists, your guide will take pictures of you posing in front of this world renown back drop. Then down into the valley for up close and personal views of Yosemite Falls (the highest waterfall in North America), Curry Village, Ansel Adams Gallery, Swinging Bridge, Yosemite Meadow and more. For lunch, let’s see what the Chef de Cuisine at Erna’s Elderberry House has created and packed up for you in your deluxe picnic basket? It will be a most memorable picnic feast including wine hand chosen from their extensive wine cellar to compliment your meal. After your picnic your personal guide will whisk you up to Glacier Point where you will enjoy the breath taking views of Half Dome, Hanging Rock and Vernal Falls. Watch the sunset, moon rise, and the galaxy of stars come out to blanket the sky for as far as the eye can see. The evening sky scape will fill you with awe and be a perfect end of your first day.

Day 3

-Start the day with another unforgettable breakfast at your villa. Then your guide will take you back to the park for a half day exploring more of Yosemite Valley which holds both forest and large open meadows, providing breathtaking views of the surrounding crests and waterfalls. Become part of the experience by taking it all in via horseback riding, cycling, or hiking. Next stop will be the world famous Ahwahnee for lunch in a dining hall that brings mother-nature indoors with floor to ceiling windows. Watch the deer and other wildlife graze on the expansive lawns, flora and fauna, while you enjoy your meal. After lunch, sweep across to Tuolomne Meadows, where you will experience breathtaking scenery and pristine lakes of the high country. The waters of Tenaya Lake and Tuolumne River soak up the heat and the sun, inviting you to swim and/or fish. This time you will exit Yosemite through the Merced River Valley, following the flow of the river back down into the foothills. There will be a point during this jaunt that you will arrive in the small historic town of Mariposa. Its authentic western, gold rush era buildings downtown, house a few excellent restaurants, winetasting rooms, craft breweries, old west saloons, and tapa bars. There you can spend the evening dining and unwinding, knowing your coach awaits you to take you back the Chateau for the night.

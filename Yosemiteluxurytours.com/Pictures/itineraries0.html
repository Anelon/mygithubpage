3 Day Kings Canyon, Sequoia, and Yosemite National Park Sample Itinerary

Day 1 Meet your professional tour guide in the Fresno Area to Wuksachi Lodge, a pinnacle of Kings Canyon Lodging, the John Muir Lodge is a stone and timber retreat located in a Grant Grove Village. Stay for the night in your deluxe room and dinner at the Peaks Restaurant. You wont find a Sequoia restaurant with a better view and elegant dinning.

Day 2 Start the day bright and early with your first visit to Kings Canyon National Park. There you will visit General Grant, a 3,000 year old wonder and center piece of the park. It measures almost 270 feet tall and 107 feet around at its base. President Calvin Coolidge named it the “Nations Christmas Tree”. Boyden Caverns is your next stop, lies beneath the massive 2,000 foot high marble walls of the famous Kings Gates. It is a magnificent cavern with many varieties of natural speleothems, including rare "shield" formations. Your next destination is Roaring River Falls, Dropping 40 feet over a narrow ledge, the falls’ rushing waters foam, froth and churn with unexpected voracity . For lunch, enjoy a picnic lunch in the wonderful peaceful Zumwalt Meadows. After your lunch take a private transfer over to Sequoia National park to finish out your day. Once arriving at the Giant Forest Museum the hub for the Park, you will be met by your private tour guide that will take you through the park passing the fallen Tunnel Log that is 17 feet wide and 8 feet tall and finishing with the General Sherman Tree, the World’s largest living organism that ascends over 300 feet tall with a circumference of over 100 feet! After finishing your trip in the Sequoias, your private transfer will take you to Fresno for a night at Comfort Inn and Suites. Once checked in, your chariot awaits for a private dinner and wine tasting to the famous Engelmann Sellers Estate, experiencing the best wine the Valley has to offer.

Day 3. Begin the day with the warm sun peaking in through your bedroom window knowing today is the day you will see on the greatest wonders the world has to offer. Escape the Valley heat with your private transfer to Yosemite Valley! There you will first experience the tunnel view where you can take photos at the most famous panorama of Yosemite Valley. You will see the iconic granite rock formation of El Capitan with Half dome rising in the background with a few peaks of cascades of breathtaking waterfalls from time to time. Next, have lunch and check in to Chateau du sureau Erna’s Elderberry House, Oakhurst California for accommodations in the Salon Suite of Villa Sureau for one night . After your mid-day break, you will be escorted to visit the scenery at the end of Glacier Point. At 3,214 feet above the valley floor, this view provides the best orange sunset glistening off the mountaintops for the last stop of the day. Just wait a little longer to experience the rising of the moon and the stars. You will be transfixed like a deer in headlights for your last night in Yosemite.
